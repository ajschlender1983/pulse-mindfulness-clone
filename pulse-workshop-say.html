Who wrote the rules

The advisory board

Five experts · one consensus

The guardrails below weren't handed down. They were argued out. Each advisor is an AI persona built to think in the voice and work of a real strategist: Alex Hormozi (offers and value), Seth Godin (remarkability and tribe), Alan Watts (presence), Steve Jobs (ruthless simplicity), and Rory Sutherland (behavioral science and perceived value, the fifth seat, added for the lens the others missed). Each judged the brand on its own, then the panel argued it out until the disagreements settled into the rules and lines here.

Portraits are stylized illustrations of the real figures, not photographs.
