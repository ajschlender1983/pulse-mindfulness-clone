Who wrote the rules

The advisory board

Five experts · ten EBI passes · one consensus

The guardrails below weren't handed down — they were argued into being. Each advisor is an AI persona built to think in the voice and body of work of a real strategist: Alex Hormozi (offers & value), Seth Godin (remarkability & tribe), Alan Watts (presence), Steve Jobs (ruthless simplicity), and Rory Sutherland (behavioral science & perceived value — the fifth seat, chosen to add the lens the others were missing). Each evaluated the brand independently, then the panel debated it across ten explore-build-improve passes until the disagreements resolved into the rules and lines you see here.

Portraits are stylized illustrations of the real figures, not photographs.
