What we say · the workshop

The writing room.

Three tools: a voice check that lints any copy against the board's guardrails, the line bench where every tagline, on-screen line, and subject gets rated and new ones get added, and the guardrails themselves. Ratings save in this browser and export as JSON.

Tool one

Voice check

Paste any copy — an email, a caption, a subject line — and it's checked against the banned vocabulary, the anti-data rule, the no-hype rule, and the "you may notice" rule.

Tool two

The line bench

The live site copy (pulled from pulsemindfulness.com), the board's consensus lines, and everything added in this room. Lines that trip a guardrail are flagged ⚠ — the Live site tab doubles as a rewrite queue. Rate each keep / maybe / cut.
