The three reasons to look

Set your intention. Deepen your practice. Find the others.

Named in text, then shown in order. First, intention: a "Daily Pulse" screen, an intention chosen — "Say yes, more" — the ring confirming it with one pulse, and a quiet, organic payoff later that morning: a colleague asks, she says yes, easily.
