How to find your size
01
Wear the sizer band overnight. Fingers change through the day and settle while you sleep. The overnight fit is the honest one.
02
Check the knuckle in the morning. The band should pass over with a little resistance, then sit without spinning. Snug, not tight.
03
Size for the hand you'll wear it on. Left and right can differ by half a size. Choose the finger first, then the number.
