Before anything else, I want to give you the thing itself. You don't need to buy anything for this to work. Not the ring, not an app, not a cushion. Just the next sixty seconds.

A 60-second practice
Feet. Feel both of them on the floor. Their weight, the press of the ground back. Fifteen seconds there.
Breath. One slow breath, out longer than in. Then one more, no better than the first.
One sound. Find the farthest sound you can hear. Stay with it until it changes.
One thing you can see. Let your eyes rest on it. Notice one detail you'd normally walk past.

That's the entire practice. No technique to learn, nothing to get right. You may notice your shoulders drop. You may notice nothing at all yet. Both count.

In fact — ten seconds, right now.
